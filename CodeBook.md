Code Book - twa

Variables
"Subject"
The subject of the test who performed the test, a group of 30 volunteers     
Integer
1 to 30

                
"Activity_Name"            
The name of the activity done
Character
WALKING
WALKING_UPSTAIRS
WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
SITTING
STANDING
LAYING
